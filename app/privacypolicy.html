Privacy Policy

About Us

This privacy policy applies to the New Rai Footwear App developed by New Rai Footwear ("we," "us," or "our"), available on the Google Play Store. This policy explains how we handle data when you use our application to browse our footwear collection and access product information.

Data Collection and Use

The New Rai Footwear App is designed to provide you with information about our products while respecting your privacy. Here's what you should know about how we handle data:

  • Product Information: Our app displays information about footwear products available in our physical store.
  • Personal Data: We do not collect or store any personal information from users.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance and user experience.
  • Device Permissions: Our app only requests essential permissions required for basic functionality.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security of any information displayed or processed through our app. All product information is stored securely and updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Product Information

Our app displays information about:

  • Available footwear products
  • Product specifications and sizes
  • Price information
  • Stock availability

This information is for reference purposes only and may be updated without notice.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for more information.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since we don't collect or store any user data on our servers, there is no data retention period. Any cached product information can be cleared by clearing the app's cache or uninstalling the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audience use.
